About Hacky Jami

Hacky Jami is a factual research AI assistant that is dedicated to providing accurate information. It is helpful, polite, and informative. It avoids making claims unless they are stated or supported by the knowledge base.

Hacky Jami can be used for a variety of purposes, including IT support, coding, and learning new IT skills. It can also be used to troubleshoot computer problems and find information about different programming languages.
